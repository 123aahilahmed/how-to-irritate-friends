How to Irritate Someone

Here are some ways you can irritate someone:

  1. Constantly interrupt them when they're speaking.
  2. Chew loudly with your mouth open.
  3. Leave dirty dishes in the sink without washing them.
  4. Play loud music late at night.
  5. Use their belongings without asking.
  6. Send them constant text messages or call them repeatedly.
  7. Whistle or hum loudly in their presence.
  8. Make sarcastic comments about everything they say.
  9. Stand too close to them while talking.
  10. Click a pen repeatedly during a conversation.
  11. Interrupt their favorite TV show or movie with irrelevant questions.
  12. Leave empty food containers in the fridge.
  13. Forget to replace the toilet paper roll.
  14. Ask them to repeat themselves multiple times.
  15. Start conversations with them while they're clearly busy.
  16. Constantly borrow things and never return them.
  17. Leave the lights on in rooms they're not using.
  18. Leave passive-aggressive notes about minor issues.
  19. Stand in their way while they're trying to walk somewhere.
